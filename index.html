🦠 The Story Telling 🦠

🧑‍💼 The aim of this datavizualization is to compare the evolution of the pandemic of COVID-19 in the world.
The maps show the proportion 📉 of vaccination 💉 on the number of coronavirus new cases in the world.
Thus, you should be able to interprate 🕵️ if vaccination helps to decrease the number of daily infections around the word.

PS. The vaccination started a bit late, so be patient to see some color on the map. 🧘
